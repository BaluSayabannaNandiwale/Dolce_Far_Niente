General Information

Our platform integrates with all major learning systems (Blackboard, Canvas, Moodle, D2L) via standard protocols or our custom API. We can also assist in transitioning paper-based exams to online delivery.

Our Live+ service offers deep customization and specialized proctor training for complex exam procedures, security measures, and test-taker interaction rules.

Compliance & Privacy

No. NocheatZone does not sell or distribute personal information for any purpose other than facilitating online proctoring. Data belongs to the institution; we are merely the service provider.

Data is stored in US-based encrypted servers (AES 256). Recordings are accessible for a period set by your institution and automatically deleted after two years following NIST guidelines.
